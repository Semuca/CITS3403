What is Loot Legends?

In 'Loot Legends,' time is both ally and adversary. As the countdown ticks away, players must swiftly gather loot from across the realm, trading strategically to level up their inventory. Every acquisition is a step closer to legendary status, but hesitation could mean defeat. Navigate the markets wisely, for in this race against time, the spoils go to the swift and the savvy. Will you seize the riches before the clock strikes zero, or be left behind in the dust of forgotten treasures?
